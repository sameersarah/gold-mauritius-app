Privacy Policy

Gold Price Mauritius ("Gold MU") · Last updated: 3 July 2026

Gold Price Mauritius is a free app published by R.H Mohamed & Fils Ltée that displays live gold prices converted to Mauritian Rupees. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — collect.

We do not collect personal information

This app does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal or device-identifying information. There is no user tracking, analytics, or advertising of any kind built into the app.

What the app does in the background

When opened, the app makes anonymous, read-only requests to two third-party public data services to display current prices:

  • api.gold-api.com — provides the live gold spot price (XAU/USD).
  • open.er-api.com — provides the live USD/MUR exchange rate.

These are standard web requests; no personal data, name, or identifier is sent as part of them. We do not control these third-party services and are not responsible for their own data practices, though to our knowledge neither service requires or collects personal information for these public endpoints.

Data stored on your device

Your karat selection (24K/22K/18K) and any local price premium adjustment you make are saved only in your device's local browser storage, so the app remembers your preference next time you open it. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or anyone else.

No ads, no third-party sharing

This app contains no advertising and does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a revised "last updated" date.
